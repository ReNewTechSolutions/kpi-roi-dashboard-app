# Billing UI Copy — Buttons, Banners, Pricing Snippets (Pro/Team)

Use this copy to keep the product “SaaS-polished” and conversion-friendly.

---

## 1) Settings → Billing Page (Recommended Layout)

### Page Title
**Billing**

### Subtitle
Manage your plan and unlock collaboration, exports, and advanced reporting.

---

## 2) Plan Cards (Copy + CTA)

### Free Plan Card
**Title:** Free  
**Tagline:** Get started with KPI tracking and ROI basics.  
**Includes (bullets):**
- 1 organization
- 1 seat
- KPI history: last 3 months
- 1 ROI scenario
- No exports

**Primary CTA (Free):**
- Button: `Upgrade to Pro`
- Secondary link: `Compare plans`

**If already Free and not logged in to billing:**
- Button: `Upgrade`

---

### Pro Plan Card
**Title:** Pro  
**Tagline:** For consultants and operators who want deeper history and exports.  
**Includes:**
- Up to 3 organizations
- Up to 3 seats
- KPI history: 24 months
- Up to 10 ROI scenarios
- CSV exports
- KPI charts

**Primary CTA (not subscribed):**
- Button: `Upgrade to Pro`

**If currently Pro:**
- Badge: `Current plan`
- Button: `Manage billing`

---

### Team Plan Card
**Title:** Team  
**Tagline:** For agencies and teams collaborating across multiple workspaces.  
**Includes:**
- Up to 10 organizations (or unlimited)
- Up to 10 seats (or unlimited)
- Unlimited KPI history
- Unlimited ROI scenarios
- CSV exports (PDF optional)
- KPI charts
- Team-ready structure

**Primary CTA (not subscribed):**
- Button: `Upgrade to Team`

**If currently Team:**
- Badge: `Current plan`
- Button: `Manage billing`

---

## 3) Top-of-Page Status Banner (Based on Subscription State)

### Active (Pro/Team)
**Banner:** Your subscription is active.  
**Subtext:** Plan changes and invoices are managed in the billing portal.

Button: `Open Billing Portal`

---

### Past Due
**Banner:** Payment issue detected.  
**Subtext:** Please update your payment method to avoid interruption.

Button: `Fix payment`

---

### Canceled (end of period)
**Banner:** Your plan will end on {DATE}.  
**Subtext:** You can renew anytime to keep access to Pro/Team features.

Button: `Renew subscription`

---

### Trialing (optional)
**Banner:** Trial active until {DATE}.  
**Subtext:** Enjoy Pro features during your trial.

Button: `Manage billing`
